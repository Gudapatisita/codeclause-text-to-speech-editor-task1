Many years ago, a king lived in a beautiful city. He had a very beautiful garden. In the garden, there was an apple tree that bore golden apples. 
The king was very particular about the number of apples growing on it. But one morning one apple was missing.King became angry. He ordered the gardener to keep an eye on the tree. 
The gardener appointed his elder son for night duty. But, he slept at midnight, and the next day another apple was gone. It made the gardener angry, and he appointed the second son for the next day. 
But, he too slept that night and one apple was gone.
